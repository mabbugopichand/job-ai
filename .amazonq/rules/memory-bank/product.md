# Product Overview

## Purpose
job-ai is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that automates job discovery, scoring, and application tracking. It scrapes job boards, uses a local LLM (Ollama) to match jobs against user profiles, and sends alerts for high-match opportunities.

## Key Features
- Automated job scraping from multiple sources (RemoteOK, Jobicy, Arbeitnow, TheMuse, Adzuna)
- AI-powered job-to-profile matching with 0–100 match scores via Ollama (gemma:2b)
- Smart alerts via email and Telegram for high-match jobs
- Application tracking with status pipeline (applied → screening → interview → offer)
- Analytics dashboard with charts on match rates, salary trends, and skill demand
- Resume upload (PDF) and profile management with skills, preferences, and salary targets
- Research job tracking (postdoc, research associate positions)
- Role-based filtering to reduce AI API costs (~36% savings)

## Target Users
Job seekers in tech (DevOps, Frontend, Backend, SRE, etc.) who want automated job discovery and AI-assisted matching instead of manual searching.

## Use Cases
- Passive job monitoring: scraper runs every 6 hours, alerts sent automatically
- Active job search: search/filter jobs, analyze match scores on demand
- Application management: track all applications and follow-ups in one place
- Market research: analytics on in-demand skills, salary ranges, and hiring trends
